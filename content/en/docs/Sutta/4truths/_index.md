## idam dukkham
- pain
- incapable of suffering
## ayam dukkha-samudayo
- origin
- arising
## ayam dukkha-nirodha

- suppress

- path, way

## This is not mine, I am not this, this is not my essence.

netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attāti—
evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.
