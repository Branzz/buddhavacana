## ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo (noble eightfold path)

```markmap
# ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo
## sammādiṭṭhi
- right belief
## sammāsaṅkappo
- right intention
## sammāvācā
- right speech
## sammākammanto
- right conduct
## sammāājīvo
- right livelihood
## sammāvāyāmo
- right effort
## sammāsati
- right memory
## sammāsamādhi
- right concentration
```
