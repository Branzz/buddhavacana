## Kinds of `sabbanāma`

1. Personal pronoun (puggalanāmaṃ).
2. Demonstrative pronoun (nidassananāmaṃ).
3. Relative pronoun (anvayīnāmaṃ).
4. Interrogative pronoun (pucchānāmaṃ).
5. Indefinite pronoun (anīyamanāmaṃ).
6. Possessive pronoun
